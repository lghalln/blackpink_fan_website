Who is Blackpink?

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment. The group consists of four members: Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. Debuting in August 2016, BLACKPINK quickly rose to international fame with their dynamic music style, combining K-pop, EDM, and hip-hop. Known for their powerful performances, fashionable aesthetics, and chart-topping hits such as "Ddu-Du Ddu-Du," "Kill This Love," and "How You Like That," BLACKPINK has become one of the most popular and influential K-pop groups globally.

THE PINKS
KIM JISOO
Position: Lead Vocalist, Visual
Birthday: January 3, 1995
Nationality: South Korean
Description: Jisoo is known for her deep and powerful vocals, as well as her stunning visuals. She often serves as the face of the group due to her charming and elegant appearance. Besides singing, Jisoo has a talent for acting and has appeared in various television dramas. She is also appreciated for her cheerful and playful personality, which endears her to fans.
THE PINKS
KIM JENNIE
Position: Main Rapper, Lead Vocalist
Birthday: January 16, 1996
Nationality: South Korean
Description: Jennie is celebrated for her versatile talent, excelling in both rap and vocal performances. She has a charismatic stage presence and is often recognized for her fashion-forward style, making her a trendsetter in the industry. Jennie debuted as a solo artist with the single "Solo" in 2018, showcasing her individuality and strong artistic identity.
THE PINKS
ROSEANNE PARK
Position: Main Vocalist, Lead Dancer
Birthday: February 11, 1997
Nationality: Korean-New Zealand
Description: Rosé is distinguished by her unique, soulful voice and exceptional live performance skills. Raised in New Zealand and Australia, she brings an international flair to the group. Rosé is also a talented instrumentalist, playing the guitar and piano. In 2021, she debuted as a solo artist with the single album "R," featuring the hit songs "On The Ground" and "Gone."
THE PINKS
LALISA MANOBAN
Position: Main Dancer, Lead Rapper, Sub Vocalist, Maknae (youngest member)
Birthday: March 27, 1997
Nationality: Thai
Description: Lisa is known for her exceptional dancing skills, often considered one of the best dancers in K-pop. Her dynamic rap delivery and captivating stage presence make her a standout performer. Originally from Thailand, Lisa adds cultural diversity to the group. She made her solo debut with the single album "LALISA" in 2021, breaking records and showcasing her talents as a solo artist.
